mango.optimize.DistributedMetricEvaluator.waitForEvaluate¶

DistributedMetricEvaluator.waitForEvaluate()¶

Loops waiting for an self.EVALUATE broadcast of the x parameter from the self.root-rank process. Exit’s loop when a (self.TERMINATE,x) broadcast is received. Should only be called by the non-self.root-rank MPI processes.
