Input/Output (mango.io)¶

This module contains functions for input/output of mango.Dds objects.

Variables¶

mango.io.BZIP2 = 'application/x-bzip2'¶

The bzip2 compression method.

mango.io.GZIP = 'application/gzip'¶

The gzip (GNU zip) compression method.
