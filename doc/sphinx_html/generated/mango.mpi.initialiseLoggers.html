mango.mpi.initialiseLoggers¶

mango.mpi.initialiseLoggers(logrNameList, handlerClass=<class 'mango.mpi.SplitStreamHandler'>, logLevel=30, rootRank=0)[source]¶

Initialises specified (by name suffix) loggers to generate output at the specified logging level. If the specified named loggers do not exist, they are created.

Parameters:
  • logrNameList (list of str) – List of logger names.
  • handlerClass (logging.Handler type.) – The handler class for output of log messages, for example SplitStreamHandler or logging.StreamHandler.
  • logLevel (int) – Log level for messages, typically one of logging.DEBUG, logging.INFO, logging.WARN, logging.ERROR or logging.CRITICAL. See Logging Levels.
  • rootRank (int) – MPI rank of the root-logger process.

See getLoggers() for example usage.
